KOVIDEX Syrup

(Dextromethorphan Hydrobromide, Phenylephrine Hydrochloride & Chlorpheniramine Maleate Syrup)

Pack Size: 60 ml / 100 ml

Composition (per 5 ml)

  • Dextromethorphan Hydrobromide I.P. – 10 mg
  • Phenylephrine Hydrochloride I.P. – 5 mg
  • Chlorpheniramine Maleate I.P. – 2 mg
  • Flavored syrupy base – q.s.

Uses

  • Dry cough (non-productive cough)
  • Common cold and flu symptoms
  • Allergic rhinitis and sneezing
  • Nasal congestion and sinus pressure
  • Watery eyes and itchy throat
  • Cough from throat irritation and minor respiratory infections

Benefits

  • Dextromethorphan: Suppresses the cough reflex at the brain level
  • Phenylephrine: Relieves nasal and sinus congestion
  • Chlorpheniramine: Manages sneezing, itching, and watery eyes
  • Triple-action formula for fast and broad relief
  • Non-sedating in most individuals at recommended doses
  • Pleasant flavor for improved compliance

Side Effects

  • Common: Drowsiness, dry mouth, dizziness, nausea, headache
  • Rare: Palpitations, allergic reactions, urinary difficulty, restlessness (esp. in children)

Safety Advice

  • Use under medical supervision, especially in children
  • Follow age-appropriate dosage as per label or physician’s instructions
  • Do not exceed the recommended dose
  • Avoid alcohol and other CNS depressants
  • Not for children under 6 years without a doctor’s advice
  • Consult a doctor if pregnant or breastfeeding
  • Use with caution in patients with hypertension, heart disease, diabetes, glaucoma, thyroid issues, or enlarged prostate

Storage Instructions

  • Store in a cool, dry place below 25°C
  • Protect from sunlight and moisture
  • Keep the bottle tightly closed after each use
  • Keep out of reach of children
