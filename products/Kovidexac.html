Kovidex AC® Syrup

(Dextromethorphan Hydrobromide, Cetirizine Dihydrochloride, Ambroxol Hydrochloride & Menthol Syrup)

Available in: 60 ml & 100 ml pack sizes

Composition (each 5 ml)

  • Dextromethorphan Hydrobromide I.P. – 10 mg
  • Cetirizine Dihydrochloride I.P. – 5 mg
  • Ambroxol Hydrochloride I.P. – 15 mg
  • Menthol I.P. – 0.5 mg
  • Flavored syrupy base – q.s.

Uses

  • Dry & productive cough
  • Allergic cough and throat irritation
  • Common cold symptoms
  • Mucus-associated respiratory congestion
  • Bronchitis and mild upper respiratory tract infections
  • Itchy throat, sneezing, and runny nose

Benefits

  • Dextromethorphan: Suppresses dry cough by acting on the brain’s cough center
  • Cetirizine: Alleviates allergy symptoms like sneezing and runny nose
  • Ambroxol: Breaks down and clears mucus, easing breathing
  • Menthol: Soothes irritated throat with a cooling effect
  • Suitable for both wet and dry cough with allergic features
  • Pleasant flavor – suitable for children and adults

Side Effects

  • Drowsiness
  • Dry mouth
  • Stomach upset
  • headache

Safety Advice

  • Use under medical supervision
  • Dosage must be appropriate to age and condition
  • Do not exceed recommended dose
  • Avoid alcohol, sedatives, and CNS depressants
  • Exercise caution in patients with asthma, heart/kidney/liver issues, or glaucoma
  • Not for children under 6 years without doctor’s advice
  • Consult a physician if pregnant or breastfeeding

Storage Instructions

  • Store in a cool and dry place below 25°C
  • Shake well before use
  • Keep tightly closed after each use
  • Protect from light and moisture
  • Keep out of reach of children
