CLOVED DUSTING POWDER

(Clotrimazole Powder 1% w/w)
Antifungal | Soothing | Odor-Control

Composition

  • Clotrimazole I.P. 1% w/w
  • Talcum base Q.S.

Uses

  • Prevention and treatment of fungal skin infections such as:
  • Athlete’s foot (Tinea pedis)
  • Jock itch (Tinea cruris)
  • Ringworm (Tinea corporis)
  • Fungal infections in body folds (intertrigo)
  • Sweat rash and prickly heat
  • Prevention of fungal infections in moist areas like underarms, groin, feet, etc.

Benefits

  • Clotrimazole 1% w/w is a broad-spectrum antifungal agent that inhibits the growth of dermatophytes, yeasts, and molds
  • Relieves itching, redness, and discomfort caused by fungal infections
  • The powder form absorbs excess moisture, keeping the skin dry and reducing fungal growth
  • Provides a cooling and soothing effect, especially in hot and humid weather
  • Helps prevent body odor and chafing caused by sweat accumulation
  • Ideal for daily use in people prone to skin infections or heavy perspiration

Side Effects

  • Itching or mild irritation at the application site
  • Redness or stinging sensation
  • Dryness or flakiness of skin
  • If any severe reaction occurs, discontinue use and consult a healthcare professional immediately.

Safety Advice

  • For external use only
  • Apply to clean, dry skin in the affected area
  • Use 2-3 times daily or as directed by a physician
  • Avoid contact with eyes, mouth, and mucous membranes
  • Do not apply on broken or severely irritated skin
  • Wash hands after use
  • Suitable for adults and children above 2 years
  • Pregnant or breastfeeding women should consult a doctor before use
  • If symptoms persist beyond 7–10 days, consult a physician

Storage Instructions

  • Store in a cool, dry place, below 25°C
  • Keep away from moisture and direct sunlight
  • Keep out of reach of children
