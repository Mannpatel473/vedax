Cloend BN Cream

(Beclomethasone Dipropionate, Neomycin Sulphate & Clotrimazole Cream)

Composition

  • Beclomethasone Dipropionate I.P. – 0.025% w/w
  • Neomycin Sulphate
    • Eq. to Neomycin I.P – 0.5% w/w
  • Clotrimazole I.P. – 1.0% w/w
  • Cream base – q.s.

Uses

  • Fungal skin infections (e.g., ringworm, athlete’s foot, jock itch)
  • Bacterial skin infections or mixed infections
  • Inflammatory skin conditions with secondary infection
  • Eczema with superadded infection
  • Seborrheic dermatitis and candidiasis
  • Intertrigo and other rashes in skin folds

Benefits

  • Beclomethasone Dipropionate: A potent corticosteroid that reduces inflammation, redness, and itching.
  • Clotrimazole: A broad-spectrum antifungal that eliminates fungal growth and prevents recurrence.
  • Neomycin Sulphate: A topical antibiotic that helps treat and prevent bacterial infections.
  • Offers broad-spectrum coverage for mixed infections (fungal + bacterial + inflammatory).
  • Fast-acting relief from itching, redness, and swelling.

Side Effects

  • Burning or stinging sensation at the application site
  • Skin dryness, irritation, or redness
  • Skin thinning or stretch marks (with prolonged use)
  • Allergic skin reactions
  • Increased hair growth at the application site (rare)

Safety Advice

  • For external use only.
  • Use only under the supervision of a qualified healthcare provider.
  • Do not use for more than 2 weeks continuously unless directed by a doctor.
  • Avoid applying on open wounds, broken skin, or face (especially near eyes or mouth).
  • Wash hands before and after application.
  • Not recommended for children under 12 years without medical advice.
  • Pregnant and breastfeeding women should consult a physician before use.
  • Do not cover with airtight dressings unless advised by your doctor.

Storage Instructions

  • Store in a cool, dry place below 25°C.
  • Protect from direct sunlight and moisture.
  • Keep out of reach of children.
