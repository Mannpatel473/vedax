ALOVED

(Aloe Vera Gel)
Natural Skin Soothing & Healing Gel

Uses

  • Moisturizing dry and rough skin
  • Soothing sunburns, rashes, and irritation
  • Healing minor cuts, wounds, and insect bites
  • Managing acne, pimples, and oily skin
  • Reducing redness and inflammation
  • Post-shaving and after-waxing skin relief
  • Scalp nourishment and dandruff control (when used on scalp)

Benefits

  • 100% natural and gentle on all skin types
  • Rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants for skin rejuvenation
  • Provides deep hydration without making the skin greasy
  • Speeds up wound healing and skin repair
  • Cools and calms irritated or inflamed skin
  • Can be used as a daily moisturizer, night gel, or after-sun care
  • Non-comedogenic (won’t clog pores) – safe for acne-prone skin
  • Leaves skin feeling soft, smooth, and refreshed

Side Effects

  • Mild burning or stinging (especially on sensitive/damaged skin)
  • Allergic reaction (rash, itching, redness – very rare)
  • Skin tightness or dryness (if used excessively without moisturizer)

Note: Always do a patch test before full use, especially on sensitive skin.

Safety Advice

  • For external use only – do not ingest
  • Apply a thin layer to clean, dry skin or scalp
  • Avoid contact with eyes, inner mouth, or open wounds
  • Discontinue use if irritation or allergic reaction occurs
  • Suitable for daily use on face, body, or scalp
  • Safe for adults, teens, and children above 3 years
  • Pregnant or breastfeeding women can use it safely unless allergic

Storage Instructions

  • Store in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight
  • Refrigeration recommended for enhanced cooling effect
  • Keep out of reach of children

Disclaimer

ALOVED is a cosmetic product for external use. While Aloe Vera is a natural remedy, it is not a substitute for medical treatment. In case of severe skin conditions, consult a dermatologist.
