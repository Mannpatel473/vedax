Pregasee

(One-Step Urine HCG Pregnancy Test Kit)

Quick | Accurate | Easy-to-Use

Uses

  • Pregasee is a home-use pregnancy test kit designed to detect the presence of hCG (human chorionic gonadotropin) hormone in urine, an early marker of pregnancy.
  • It is used for:
    • Confirming pregnancy at home
    • Early detection of pregnancy after a missed period
    • Convenient self-testing without visiting a clinic
    • Can be used at any time of the day (preferably first-morning urine for best results)

Side Effects

  • Pregasee is a non-invasive diagnostic tool and has no physical side effects. However, users should be aware of:
    • False negatives (test shows negative even when pregnant):
      • Testing too early
      • Diluted urine
      • Incorrect usage
    • False positives (test shows positive without pregnancy):
      • Recent miscarriage or abortion
      • Certain medications (containing hCG)
      • Medical conditions (very rare)
