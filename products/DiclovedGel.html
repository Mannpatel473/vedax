Dicloved® Gel

(Diclofenac Diethylamine, Linseed oil, Methyl Salicylate & Menthol Gel)

Composition

  • Diclofenac Diethylamine B.P.
    • eq. to Diclofenac Sodium - 1.16% w/w
  • Linseed Oil B.P. – 3% w/w
  • Methyl Salicylate I.P. – 10% w/w
  • Menthol I.P. – 5% w/w
  • Benzyl alcohol I.P. – 1% w/w (as preservative)
  • Gel base – q.s.

Uses

  • Muscle pain and stiffness
  • Joint pain due to arthritis or injury
  • Back pain and sprains
  • Sports injuries (e.g., tendonitis, strains, bruises)
  • Cervical spondylosis and frozen shoulder
  • Mild nerve pain and inflammation

Benefits

  • Fast-acting pain relief at the application site
  • Reduces inflammation, swelling, and stiffness
  • Non-greasy formula with quick absorption
  • Cooling and warming sensation from menthol and methyl salicylate
  • Linseed oil enhances penetration and has anti-inflammatory effects
  • Minimizes systemic side effects associated with oral painkillers

Side Effects

  • Mild skin irritation
  • Redness
  • Itching
  • Burning
  • Rash or allergic reaction

Safety Advice

  • For external use only. Do not apply to broken or infected skin
  • Avoid contact with eyes, mouth, and mucous membranes
  • Wash hands after use (unless treating hands)
  • Do not tightly bandage or use heat over applied area
  • Not for use in children under 12 years unless directed by a doctor
  • Consult doctor before use in pregnancy or breastfeeding
  • Avoid use with other topical anti-inflammatory products

Storage

  • Store in a cool, dry place below 25°C
  • Protect from sunlight and heat
  • Keep out of reach of children
