Cafimost Tablets

Composition

  • Acetaminophen (Paracetamol) IP – typically 500 mg
  • Caffeine IP – typically 30-65 mg (verify exact strength on packaging)

Uses

  • Relief of mild to moderate pain (headache, migraine, toothache, muscle aches, menstrual pain, back pain)
  • Fever reduction
  • Fatigue-related headaches (due to caffeine’s stimulant effect)

Benefits

  • Faster pain relief: Caffeine enhances the effect of acetaminophen
  • Improved alertness: Caffeine reduces drowsiness
  • Convenient combination: One tablet addresses both pain and fatigue
  • Commonly well-tolerated when used short-term at correct doses

Side Effects

  • Common: Nausea, upset stomach, restlessness or nervousness (caffeine-related), insomnia (if taken late)
  • Rare but serious: Liver damage (with acetaminophen overdose), increased heart rate or palpitations (caffeine-related), allergic reactions (rash, itching, swelling)

Safety Advice

  • Take after food to reduce stomach upset
  • Stay within recommended dose (max 4g acetaminophen daily)
  • Drink plenty of water
  • Keep 4–6 hours between doses
  • Do not combine with alcohol (risk of liver toxicity)
  • Avoid excessive caffeine-containing products
  • Do not use with other acetaminophen-containing medicines
  • Avoid long-term use without doctor consultation
  • Caution in liver disease, kidney disease, heart problems, pregnancy, or breastfeeding – consult doctor

Storage

  • Keep in a cool, dry place
  • Away from children and sunlight
