Dicloved G Tablets

Composition

  • Diclofenac Sodium I.P. – 50 mg
  • Paracetamol I.P. – 325 mg

Uses

  • Musculoskeletal pain (e.g., back pain, neck pain, sprains)
  • Arthritic conditions (e.g., osteoarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis)
  • Post-surgical pain
  • Dental pain
  • Headaches and migraines
  • Menstrual cramps

Benefits

  • Dual-action relief: Diclofenac provides anti-inflammatory and analgesic effects, Paracetamol enhances pain relief and reduces fever
  • Faster onset of action for pain management
  • Effective in both acute and chronic pain conditions
  • Helps improve mobility and quality of life during painful episodes

Side Effects

  • Common: Gastric discomfort, nausea, vomiting, indigestion, dizziness, headache, skin rash, or itching
  • Prolonged use: Liver enzyme changes
  • Serious (rare): Stomach ulcers/bleeding, liver toxicity, kidney dysfunction, allergic reactions (e.g., swelling, breathing difficulty)

Safety Advice

  • Do not exceed the recommended dose
  • Take with food or after meals to reduce gastric irritation
  • Avoid alcohol during use
  • Not suitable for patients with peptic ulcers, severe liver/kidney issues, or NSAID allergies
  • Use caution in elderly due to gastrointestinal and cardiovascular risk
  • Pregnant/breastfeeding women should consult a doctor
  • Avoid concurrent use with other NSAIDs or paracetamol-containing products

Storage

  • Store in a cool, dry place below 25°C
  • Protect from direct sunlight and moisture
  • Keep out of reach of children
