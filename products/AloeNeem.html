ALOE NEEM Soap

(Neem & Aloe Vera Herbal Soap)

Natural Antibacterial & Skin-Soothing Formula

Uses

  • Daily cleansing of oily, acne-prone, and sensitive skin
  • Controlling pimples, rashes, and skin infections
  • Reducing body odor and excess oil
  • Soothing skin irritations, itching, and redness
  • Preventing fungal and bacterial skin conditions
  • Gentle cleansing for all skin types, especially teenagers

Benefits

  • Neem Extract: Antibacterial, antifungal, and anti-inflammatory – helps fight acne and skin infections
  • Aloe Vera Extract: Deeply hydrates, cools, and supports skin healing
  • Removes excess oil and impurities without drying the skin
  • Maintains clear, smooth, and refreshed skin
  • Herbal and chemical-free base – suitable for daily use on face and body
  • Leaves skin feeling soft, clean, and naturally glowing

Side Effects

  • Generally safe – made from natural ingredients
  • Rare: Mild dryness, tightness, or irritation (especially if allergic to Neem or Aloe)
  • Slight stinging if used on open wounds

Safety Advice

  • For external use only
  • Avoid contact with eyes, mouth, or broken skin
  • If contact with eyes occurs, rinse thoroughly with water
  • Discontinue if burning, rash, or allergy develops
  • Suitable for men, women, and teenagers
  • Safe for daily use on face and body

Storage Instructions

  • Store in a cool, dry place away from sunlight
  • Allow soap to dry between uses to extend shelf life
  • Keep out of reach of children

Disclaimer

ALOE NEEM is a natural personal care product. While safe for regular use, it is not a substitute for medical treatment. For persistent skin issues, consult a dermatologist.
