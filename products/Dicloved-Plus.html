Dicloved Plus Tablets

Diclofenac Potassium & Paracetamol Tablets

Composition (Each uncoated tablet contains)

  • Diclofenac Potassium B.P. – 50 mg
  • Paracetamol (Acetaminophen) I.P. – 325 mg
  • Excipients – Q.S.

Uses

  • Musculoskeletal pain (back pain, neck pain, sprains)
  • Joint pain & arthritis
  • Toothache & dental pain
  • Post-surgical pain
  • Menstrual cramps (dysmenorrhea)
  • Headaches & migraines
  • Fever (temporary relief)

Benefits

  • Dual action pain relief: Diclofenac reduces inflammation; Paracetamol relieves pain & fever
  • Faster onset: Diclofenac potassium form is faster-acting than diclofenac sodium
  • Synergistic effect: Combined action provides better relief than either drug alone
  • Useful in acute flare-ups of arthritis or injury

Side Effects

  • Gastric irritation or acidity
  • Nausea
  • Dizziness
  • Heartburn

Safety Advice

  • Use only under the supervision of a qualified healthcare provider.
  • Take after meals to protect stomach
  • Maintain at least 6-hour gap between doses
  • Hydrate well – supports kidney function
  • Use short-term unless advised by a doctor
  • Avoid alcohol – increases risk of liver & stomach damage
  • Don’t use with other NSAIDs
  • Don’t take other paracetamol products together – risk of overdose
  • Avoid long-term use without medical supervision
  • Caution in stomach ulcers or acid reflux, liver or kidney disease, heart conditions or high BP, elderly patients, pregnant or breastfeeding women – consult doctor first

Storage

  • Store in a cool, dry place
  • Keep out of reach of children
  • Protect from moisture and direct sunlight
