OMItrend D® Capsules

(Omeprazole & Domperidone Capsules I.P.)

Composition (Each capsule contains)

  • Omeprazole I.P. – 20 mg (as enteric-coated pellets)
  • Domperidone I.P. – 10 mg (as sustained-release pellets)
  • Excipients – Q.S.

Uses

  • Gastroesophageal reflux disease (GERD)
  • Acid reflux & heartburn
  • Non-ulcer dyspepsia (indigestion)
  • Peptic and duodenal ulcers
  • Nausea and vomiting associated with acid reflux or delayed gastric emptying
  • Zollinger-Ellison syndrome (hyperacidity condition)

Benefits

  • Omeprazole reduces gastric acid secretion by blocking proton pumps, providing long-lasting relief from acidity and heartburn.
  • Domperidone enhances gastric motility and helps in faster stomach emptying, reducing symptoms of bloating, nausea, and reflux.
  • Dual-action formula offers rapid symptom relief and healing of gastric mucosa.
  • Prevents relapse of acid-related symptoms when used regularly as prescribed.
  • Especially effective for GERD-related regurgitation, burping, and nausea.

Side Effects

  • Headache
  • Abdominal pain or bloating
  • Dry mouth
  • Diarrhea or constipation
  • Dizziness or drowsiness

Safety Advice

  • Take 15–30 minutes before meals, usually once or twice daily as prescribed.
  • Swallow the capsule whole – do not crush or chew.
  • Not recommended for long-term use without medical supervision.
  • Use cautiously in patients with liver or kidney impairment or history of cardiac arrhythmia.
  • Pregnant or breastfeeding women should use only under a doctor’s advice.
  • Avoid alcohol and smoking, which can worsen acidity and reflux.
  • Inform your doctor if you're taking antifungals, antibiotics, anticoagulants, or antiepileptics.

Storage Instructions

  • Store in a cool, dry place below 25°C.
  • Keep away from moisture and direct sunlight.
  • Keep out of reach of children.
